    • King’s Crown Shakepeare Troupe is an acting company dedicated to delivering free, quality theater to the Columbia University community. For their fall 2017 season, KCST featured a production of Euripides' Medea.

    As the graphic designer, I found inspiration in director Asya Sagnak’s description of the set design as containing imagery of flowers and a pomegranate. The bright colours and bold shapes alongside the historical typefaces reflect the production's contemporary setting while keeping the text in antiquity. The design was also modified for print, social media and t-shirts for the crew.
