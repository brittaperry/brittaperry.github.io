    • Free Friday Films is a film program hosted by University of Toronto’s Cinema Studies Student Union. As the Art Director of the organization, I designed visual assets to promote screenings for print and social media.

    The design, featuring headings for each month, was inspired by posters I encountered in Rome and Lucerne for daily outdoor screenings in the summer. Using a simple colour palette, the posters can be cheaply reproduced with risograph while maintaining a bold, eye-catching look.

    In addition to the schedule, I also created individual posters and cover photos for films each week for print and social media promotion.
