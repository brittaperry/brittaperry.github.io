    • Camera Stylo is University of Toronto's annual undergraduate cinema studies journal, featuring six papers selected by student editors and a staff advisor.

    As the sole layout designer of the 88-page publication, I worked closely with the editor-in-chief to lay out academic papers while maintaining a consistent citation format with endnotes. With the launch date in mind, I completed this project on InDesign with a turnaround time of one week.

    To maintain interest and variation, I introduced title pages with film stills overlaid with a color halftone filter for each section. The cover is a blown-up image of popcorn with a red overlay inspired by Rachel Gao's opening letter from the editor, which ended with "See you at the movies."
